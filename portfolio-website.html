Case 03

Brand Content & Visuals: STABLI Revamp

Core skill

Translating technical terms into consumer-friendly content

Transforming technical product descriptions into language that resonates with customer pain points, driving engagement and purchase intent.

Before: technical terms overload
"Short foot exercise – strengthens plantar muscles (abductor hallucis, flexor hallucis brevis, quadratus plantae…) helps maintain arch shape…"

Problem: Too many terms create distance; customers don't understand what to do.

After: solution-oriented

Short foot exercise – Strengthens foot muscles, helps slow the progression of bunions.

Goal: Build strength in the foot muscles, maintain the arch, and reduce pressure on the big toe.

Win: Clear pain points, logical flow, increased in-store experience and purchase intent.
